Physics notes

  • Force law: F = G m₁ m₂ r̂ / r² with Plummer softening r² → r²+ε².
  • Mechanical energy E = K + U is conserved by Newtonian gravity. Integrators only approximate this.
  • Velocity Verlet is symplectic: long-term energy error stays bounded for fixed Δt.
  • Forward Euler is not — energy typically grows and orbits spiral outward.
